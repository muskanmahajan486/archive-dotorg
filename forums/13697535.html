Comments:

OpenRemote Controller connects to KNX or X10 gateways, not to devices directly, and we don't impose particular limitations on number of devices.

The addressing of KNX or X10 protocols themselves may impose limits though, X10 has space for 256 devices in its address space – KNX address space can be expanded with bridges across networks so generally you can go above 256 devices.

You can use both KNX and X10 if you want. Differences are mainly in price and reliability. KNX is very expensive in residential installations but generally very reliable as it is used a lot in commercial and industrial setups. It is difficult for do-it-yourself users. X10 is cheap but not highly regarded in terms of reliability, most consider it suitable for getting-started and simple test scenarios for few devices but not reliable enough for fully automated home.
