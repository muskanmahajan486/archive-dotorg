Hi Tommy,

If you're happy compiling the Android APK then you can take this branch from this repo: -

svn+ssh://kurrazyman@svn.code.sf.net/p/openremote/code/workspace/richturner/Android_Console_2_0_0_Betas

Then change line 17 of the AndroidManifest.xml to: -

android:icon="@drawable/ic_launcher_openremote" android:label="@string/app_name" android:theme="@android:style/Theme.Black.NoTitleBar">

Compile and install.
