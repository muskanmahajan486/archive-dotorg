Comments:

Trunk is fine to look at if you're just looking to get familiar with the codebase.

Development occurs in each developer's individual workspaces.

There are unassigned tasks in jira.openremote.org although they mostly assume you're already quite familiar with the codebase (see point about trunk).

If there's a particular task you want to take on while browsing trunk just ping us here and we'll help you forward.
