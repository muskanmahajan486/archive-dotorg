I have a rasberry here that I'm considering using to host my OR controller. (Instructions here for those interested: http://www.openremote.org/display/docs/OpenRemote+2.0+How+To+-+Z-Wave+with+Razberry)

In order to take real advantage of the rasberry, it is probably wise to purchase the Razberry as well. This is the zwave chip with accompanying software made by the Ras folks. I am curious whether, after purchasing a Razberry and using the rasberry as my OR controller host, is there any reason to purchase the aeon zstick? From what I understand the Razberry will serve the function of the zstick.

Comments:

RaZberry is a "Zstick + Zway software" so you won't need to buy an aeon stick. A similar USB device + Zway for Windows will be available in EU soon.
There has recently been a batch of ill functioning RaZberry's. They have been recalled by the manufacturer. So do check with your distributor before purchasing.
