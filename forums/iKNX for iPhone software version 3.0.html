The problem that OR KNX cannot reconnect to the IP gateway is due to the crash. Due to that OR KNX could not disconnect properly from the gateway which as a consequence denies a second (in its eyes) connection from your device. Typically (at least my Siemens N148) needs one or two minutes to delete you connection internally if you no longer react to the keep-alive packets.
