There isn't one supported as part of our package at the moment, but some users have integrated text-to-speech on their own: Geek Grand Dad - Spoken Alerts from OpenRemote.

It is using the free freetts synthesizer.

Since freetts is a Java package, integrating it is quite easy (the geek grand dad blog gives the code snippets) but since it is not part of our distribution yet, these programming steps are still required. Depending how involved you can get with Java, this may or may not be an option for you at the moment.

Anyway, I would welcome a patch submission from anyone who'd be interested in hooking that in. The steps are pretty well described in the blog, just some wrapping up to do.
