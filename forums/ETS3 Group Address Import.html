Hello Marcus;
I saw that you've added the ETS4 project import somewhere on the forum. Nice.
ETS3 have the same posiibilities both to export projects and to export group addresses in various text formats.
The default format is three columns with main /middle /sub group separated. You can choose to /not to export header information. "TAB" separator is default, but you can choose to use comma or semicolon as well.

Here is an exaple;

"Main" "Middle" "Sub" "Address"
"Lys" "0//"
"Etg U" "0/0/-"
"Bad - Taklys AV/PÅ" "0/0/1"
"Bad - Taklys Dimming" "0/0/2"
"Bad - Taklys Status" "0/0/3"
"Bad - Lys over speil AV/PÅ" "0/0/4"
"Bad - Lys over speil Status" "0/0/5"
"Bad - Lys Automatisk AV/PÅ" "0/0/6"
"Boblebad - Lys Sone 1 AV/PÅ" "0/0/7"
"Boblebad - Lys Sone 1 Dimming" "0/0/8"
"Boblebad - Lys Sone 1 Status" "0/0/9"
"Boblebad - Lys Sone 2 AV/PÅ" "0/0/10"
"Boblebad - Lys Sone 2 Dimming" "0/0/11"
"Boblebad - Lys Sone 2 Status" "0/0/12"
