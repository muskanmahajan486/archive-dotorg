One of the things I struggled with on Openremote is making a GUI that works well on all OSes and screen sizes.

I eventually gave up and settled on just making a iPad iOS version that would sit on the wall.

Now there is an app called HomeRemote which promises to build apps for Android, iOS and Windows!

I just started using it but so far it seems workable, there are a few hits and misses here and there but it's a good start.

Just use the JSON outputted from OpenRemote and you can add it to this app.

Hopefully you guys find it useful and let me know how you go
