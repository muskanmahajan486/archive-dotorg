It is easy. With UI editor, place a slider widget somewhere on the screen. You only have to find/make + and minus images. Use that for up and down images. Use an Empty.png for the other three images.
If your designer does not have Empty.png just make a 1*1 image.
Final step is to size down the height/width of the slider to make the positioning optimal.
Your rules solution probably gives a nicer spacing and choice of buttons.

I did use this for setting the start time for a dishwasher
