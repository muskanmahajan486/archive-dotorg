Hi Jason,

That is very annoying, not sure about getting back your previous screens but chances are not I would say.

A couple of points though, if you have the option I would definitely use a webkit based browser such as Chrome or Safari as these seem to work with GWT (which is what the designer is written in) a lot better.

If you do suddenly see some weird things going on in the designer close the window and log back in as this will limit any corruption as the designer frequently auto saves.

The bugs/limitations of the current designer are clear to everyone and it needs a big update (as it was written with a long outdated version of GWT), this will likely be something I pick up once I get the Web Console 2.0 released.
