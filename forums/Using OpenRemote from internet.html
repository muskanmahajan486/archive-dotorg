Yes, you can - I'm doing it all the time. Basically three options are available:

1. Configure port forwarding and use dyndns service for your home IP, or
2. Create VPN connection with your home LAN, or
3. Use global IPV6 address.

For me the last option is the best. As not always IP6 is available, I've created ipv6 tunnel through sixxs.org
