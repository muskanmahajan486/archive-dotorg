Posted by mredeker at Dec 27, 2013 22:24

Try http://192.168.1.104:8060/keypress/Home
With an uppercase "H" as in Home (may not be the case but try it)
It works for me.

Also ensure the developer mode is enabled in your Roku by pressing the following sequence in quick succession in your IR remote

3x Home, 2x Up, Right, Left, Right, Left, Right

A screen should come up asking if you want to enable it.
