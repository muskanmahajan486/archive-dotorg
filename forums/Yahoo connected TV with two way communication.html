Yahoo introduces two way communication to their connected TV platform. This would mean that there are ways of communicating with the TV beyond IR. Yahoo Connected TV is a feature of TV's from Samsung, LG and others.

This device communication platform supports two-way message-passing
between Internet-enabled devices and Connected TVs running Yahoo!
Connected TV. Keyboard, navigation, and TV App specific messages can be
communicated through a new protocol over a local network.
Here are a few examples:
*Mobile Phones as Smart Remote Controls* \- Use an Android  phone or an iPhone as a smart remote control for a Yahoo\! Connected TV,  taking advantage of the phone's superior searching, sorting,  navigation, and data entry controls.

*Tablet Devices for Data Rich Interfaces* \- Use the  Yahoo\! Connected TV companion app on the iPad to connect with &nbsp;the  currently playing TV program. Access a rich set of web results on &nbsp;the  tablet which can be played on the TV with the touch of a button.

*TV Displays for Shared Web Viewing* \- Access web videos on a tablet and send them to a big screen TV for shared viewing.
