IIRC no work has been done on the native openremote z-wave implementation. On past experience I do not expect anything happening soon.
The most viable solution is to use it with Micasaverde Vera. I am presently using OpenRemote 2.0 How To - Control Radiator - Stella Z with RazBerry. At the moment I would not recommend RaZberry for new installations, as it is still very unstable, while user support is minimal.

If you want to just play first with the Aeon stick and the Stella, you may consider the free Z-Cloud service.
