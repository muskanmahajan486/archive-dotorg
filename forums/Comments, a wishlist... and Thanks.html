Possible additions to a "Newbie" FAQ:
------------------------------------------
(Things that would have helped me anyway... If these exist elsewhere, I did not find them.)

  • How do you access/get to the setup/server connection screen when using a iPad?

Program in a settings button that links to the Navigation -> Action -> Settings.
I had things working on the iPad for a while with no way to get to the Settings/
Server screen. On an Android tablet it was easy. On the iPad is was not as obvious.

I have to admit, this took me longer to figure out than it should have.

  • How do you put one image on top of another without losing the first image?

Use a background image as your base. You can then add an image on top of that.

When I first started, I would upload a picture (a remote control) and then try to add
a button over it. As soon as you click off of the button, it causes your first picture
to be moved to the foreground... causing your second picture to "disappear".

  • How do you replace a graphics image once you've uploaded it?

You can replace it on/within your local controller -> webapps/controller/*.png
I had created a small tranparent .png to use as the background for a button.
I had messed it up slightly by not making the entire image transparent (I did
not notice this at first). I wanted to replace that image without changing its
name - and not having to update a bunch of buttons that were using it.

Is there a better answer to this?

same. It's a pain to have to create two sets of Devices with the same IR commands,
just to change the location they are going to (i.e. 2 different IP's).

  • Better resolution Android graphics. The current resolution is poor in comparison to the iPad.
