I use X10 devices with OpenRemote with no problems. I am not aware of any ethernet X10 devices. See http://www.openremote.org/display/docs/OpenRemote 2.0 How To - X10 Devices. I use the CM11A X10 interface connected to my OpenRemote server.

I also use the telnet and shell execution protocols in OpenRemote to send messages to my HouseBot server which then sends X10 commands if appropriate. I plan to slowly replace HouseBot completely with OpenRemote though.

Ravi,

I use the X10 protocol in OR (in addition to shell and telnet). Never had a problem with it and was very easy to implement. Just create a command and tie it to a button. I am not at home right now and my memory is a little foggy as to how I have things set up but send me some screen shots of your OR settings and I'll compare them to mine.

X10 devices (with a couple of rare exceptions) do not support any kind of status reporting/response. I use a sensor linked to a switch but that only tells you if OR thinks the X10 device is on or off based on the switch. It's not a true status of the X10 device itself.
