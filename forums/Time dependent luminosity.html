Art Balourdas says:This may help: Cron expressions are comprised of 6 required fields and one opt...

Here the cron jobs are well explained..

So in short your rules should look like:

package org.openremote.controller.model.event
global org.openremote.controller.statuscache.CommandFacade execute;
global org.openremote.controller.statuscache.SwitchFacade switches;
import org.openremote.controller.protocol.*;

rule "turn on light at 60%"

timer (cron: 0 0 21-7 ? * *)

when

eval(true)
Switch (source == "L18", value == "on")

then

execute.command("L18 at 60%");

end
