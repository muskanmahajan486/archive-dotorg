Hello,

I explained in more detail to Frederic in private email but the short version of it is that we can only make the source available to you privately if you're ok with signing a contractor agreement which extends a non-disclosure of the Z-wave SDK to you.

Sigma Designs have made it very clear that any open source implementation of Z-wave is against their terms and wishes. Therefore we can only make the source available to those who agree with these terms (very unfortunate but we cannot do anything else).

So let me know if signing a non-disclosure is an option for you and we can extend the source access to you. Feel free to email me with further details at juha at openremote dot org
