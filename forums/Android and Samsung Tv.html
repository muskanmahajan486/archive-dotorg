Hi Jo

Maybe I can help.

You didn't install the controller on your Phone and Tablet What you probably did is install the Apps on your Phone and Tablet, but you certainly didn't install the controller.

Let me explain. The system works by having your phone and tablet talk back to a PC (or Server PC). The Wifi Control Signals do not go from your phone/tablet to your Samsung TV, they have to go from your Phone/Tablet back to the PC running the OpenRemote Controller and then the PC running the Controller sends out the TCP/IP to the Samsung TV.

So you have to have a PC running in the background all of the time.

The Apps on your phone and tablet need to be configured in the settings to find the PC running the Controller (e.g. 192.168.1.x:8080/controller), then in your panel design for each command you set a TCP/IP string for your Samsung. So you press a button, and TCP/IP command is sent back to your PC, and your PC then sends the command to the Samsung.

Does that help?
