Posted by biomaynard at Dec 16, 2013 23:10

For accessing your controller from outside you can do either:
1. configure your router port forwarding to make the port 8080 visible through your public IP address;
2. configure VPN access to your local network (many routers or NAS have VPN servers). When running VPN server your controller would have the same LAN address.

You can use either of these solutions, depending which one works for you better.

As Michal said you have to replace localhost with your public IP address or domain name for example:

http://jonjonas.org:8080/webconsole

or in your android specify

http://jonjonas.org:8080/controller

The controller continues to run on your local machine
