Here is a snippet how I do it. 

    <command id="23" protocol="socket">
      <property name="port" value="21000" />
      <property name="command" value="Asnd apple,right" />
      <property name="ipAddress" value="192.168.100.11" />
    </command>

My remote name is "apple" and it has a button "right". It has to be the database version.
You don't need irserver if you have the device with database.
