In your controller's /conf/server.xml file, find the section:

<Service name="Catalina">

And add:

    <Connector executor="HTTP-ThreadPool"
               port="9090" protocol="HTTP/1.1"
               connectionTimeout="20000"
               redirectPort="8443" />

Note that the auto-discovery will currently only advertize a single port for connection, you will need to configure the alternative port manually in the console.
