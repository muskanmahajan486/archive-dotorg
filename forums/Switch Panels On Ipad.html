Found it.

If you want to switch between some test panels on the Ipad. Just stop the OpenRemote service on the Synology Nas and restart the OpenRemote app on the Ipad. Then the app
starts to complain it can't find the controller. Then restart the service on the Nas and then the app on the Ipad can find the controller and you are able to change
to another designed panel.

To switch panels on the iPad, you need to be able to get back to the Settings screen from your design.
You can either do that by using a "special" navigation to the settings page. In the designer, e.g. on a button, tick the "Add Navigation" check box, then select "setting" from the "Action" dropdown.

If you forget and don't want such a button, shake your device and it'll bring you to the settings screen.
