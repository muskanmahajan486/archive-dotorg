Hi,

Make sure that your custom panel is the exact same dimensions as your screen size (1080x1920) then the panel you see in the designer should match your device, also check you haven't set a X & Y scale factor in the settings for the controller you are connecting to (both values should be 1.0 if your designer panel matches your screen size exactly).
