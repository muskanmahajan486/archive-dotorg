That could an issue. If your controller is not running on port 8080 but your android discovered it on 8080, it cannot communicate.
Try and change the "webapp.port=8080" setting to "webapp.port=80" in the file "webapps/conntroller/WEB-INF/classes/config.properties" of your controller and restart.
