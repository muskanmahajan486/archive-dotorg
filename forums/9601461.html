Posted by fabulous at May 29, 2010 07:19

the knx gateway is a gateway between the eib protocol and tcp/ip.
When you installed this module you configured the tcp/ip parameters.
One of these parameters is the default gateway. Usually it is the address of your router. If this parameter is not correctly setup, your module cannot "speak" to ip addresses outside of your private lan.
