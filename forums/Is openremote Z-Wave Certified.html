Posted by joe.s at Feb 25, 2013 14:47

Z-Wave certification does not mean a lot. There are some manufacturers that have only part of their products certified, which makes customers think all of them are certified. You have to check that!
The Zwave alliance claims that Z-Wave certified products work well with certified products from other manufacturers. The fora of different home automation products (MicasaVerde, Homeseer, etc.) do tell a different story.
At the moment Openremote has very limited native support for Z-Wave. There is work ongoing to interface with MicasaVerde Vera, which seems a viable interim solution. Razzberry+Pi from Zwave.me looks like an interesting gateway to.
