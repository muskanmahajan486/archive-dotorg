Posted by pz1 at Jul 01, 2013 06:35

Eventually it will but this won't change in minor releases since it will disrupt upgrade path for any existing users who are currently running and have their designer configuration assuming port 8080.

So it will need to wait for another major update or introduction of migration tools that automates the change for most users – whichever comes first. I prefer the least amount of surprises between upgrades.

Alternative packages (Pro, Synology, etc.) are of course free to change to IANA port already. Also there's a task ORCJAVA-285 which can reduce the issue by improving the reporting of port conflict to the user, and even potentially suggesting an alternative port (8688).
