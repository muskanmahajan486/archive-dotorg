GC vs. IRTrans won't make a huge difference, fundamentally they do the same thing. Your choice may be based on some of the specifics or price though.

You won't need Apple Developer license to install the released panel rendered on your iPhone. If you plan on doing development, then having a dev account probably makes sense.

The networking questions really depend how you want to set things up. There are multiple ways to go about it. Networks can be bridged, you can add a switch or a router, etc.
