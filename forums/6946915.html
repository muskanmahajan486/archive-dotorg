Hello John,

As far as I understand, NK1 and NK2 use older EIBlib protocol for connecting KNX to ethernet. EIBlib is not part of the KNX specification and is not supported. The Siemens router on the other hand does implement the KNXnet/IP protocol as per the specification.

For OpenRemote KNX, see the list of tested gateways here:
ListOfGateways
Known problems

For OpenRemote Boss, the same information is here:
Configure OpenRemote Boss 1.0 for KNX

(see the FAQ section on information about NK1 & NK2)
