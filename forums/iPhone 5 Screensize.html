Sorry for very late answer, looking for some older information in the forums and just now noticing this post.

The iOS console does not support Retina configurations yet. This means you need to use the "point" size of the iOS device screen.
iPhone 3.5" screen (retina or not) : 320 x 480
iPhone 4" screen : 320 x 568
All iPads (retina or not) : 768 x 1024
