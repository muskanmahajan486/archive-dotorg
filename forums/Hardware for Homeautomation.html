Comments:

For KNX, the flow is from button press on your mobile, to a controller which uses IP network to reach a KNX gateway. The KNX gateway then translates the IP message to an KNX bus message (bus is a KNX wiring that has been installed to your house in this case).

There's no IR involved in this scenario.

With regards to hardware, the answer unfortunately is 'it depends'. There's no single hardware solution that is good at solving all areas of home automation including light control, TV control and HVAC.

You could choose for example KNX or Lutron for lighting and they've got fairly decent HVAC integration options too. For TV control it depends on your TV model, most common is still infrared control in which case something like an IRTrans or GlobalCache could be useful.

Once you have all these pieces together (lights, HVAC, A/V), you can use OpenRemote to integrate them and control all of them from a single unified interface (iOS, Android, Web, etc.)

Hope this helps,
