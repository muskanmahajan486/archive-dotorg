Posted by jukkevp at Apr 26, 2011 17:37

To find your iPad's (or iPhone's) UDID, do the following-
1. Search for "UDID" in the Apple App Store on your device
2. Several apps should show up in your search. Pick one and install it (I've used UDID and UDID Tool)
3. Launch the app and send Eric the UDID as shown in the application. The UDID will look like a long string of random letters and numbers.

Make sure you're doing all of this from the iPad on which you want to run the OpenRemote application. Each Apple iDevice has a unique UDID and developer pre release builds are tied to specific UDIDs.

As far as how to contact Eric, I don't know if he gives out his direct email address. The best thing to do would probably be to try and get his attention here in the forums.
