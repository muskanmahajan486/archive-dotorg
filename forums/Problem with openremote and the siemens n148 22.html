Hi Peter,

Had a look at your design. The only thing that was slightly different is that your write commands are going to group address 1/1/30 where as your status is read from 1/1/31.

Was this intentional?

The second mismatch I noticed is that in the original post you mentioned reading status from group address 1/1/13 instead of 1/1/31... so check to make sure your group addresses are indeed what you intended?

This error: 

[Controller]2011-01-15 00:18:56,509 INFO [Thread-8] org.openremote.controller.statuscache.StatusCache.saveOrUpdateStatus(68) | Status is null or "" while calling saveOrUpdateStatus in statusCache.

In short means that status cannot be read from 1/1/31 group address (or at least it cannot be resolved into the one-bit DPT 1.xxx value).

If you remove the status part from your design (the sensor and use of group address 1/1/31), do the individual button to sent light on or off still work (which would exclude an issue with the gateway itself).
