Hi all,
I am a newcomer to home automation. Actually I have no experience yet whatsoever, however, I am intrigued by the possibilities.
For a newcomer like me, it would be incredibly useful if there would be something like a comparison matrix which would give an overview of advantages and disadvantages of the different existing standards. The more choice one has, the more time it takes to come to a decision. So in general I think it is useful to offer guidance in this process.
I was thinking that such a matrix should at least contain information on whether or not a standard is well documented; whether or not it is supported by openremote; and advantages/disadvantages of the standard, such as the possibility of interference and other pros and cons of the standard (as a newcomer I do not yet have a good idea of what is important).
I think it would be best if such a matrix would be relatively short and simple. Explanations of why certain qualities are a pro or con can be listed below the table.
It would also be nice if the table would link to a list with manufacturers, so that as soon as someone has made an informed decision regarding the standard he wants to use, it is possible to see which manufacturers make products using the standard.
At the moment I do not have any devices that can be used in a home automation system and this type of information might help me and other newcomers to finally enter this interesting domain.
Bolawy
