If you login into dropbox with a web browser and go to "Settings->My Apps", you should see "OpenRemote KNX" as an App registered to your account.
Once you delete the App there, the iOS App throws an error and presents the login box again where you can enter dropbox username and password.

Since you wrote in your first post, that you deleted the dropbox account and you want to use a new one, I am not sure how that will work. I would assume that the iOS App should also show you the dropbox login window again, since your account is deleted.
