As far as I know, iPhone should work the same that the iPAD, so I'll try to give you an generic overview:
OpenRemote is working with the Designer, the once you' ve used to create the navigation & UI part.
There is also the Controller. the main application that needs to run on a "server". You can download it from: http://www.openremote.org/display/docs/Product+Overview
Once you've installed the controller, you've to connect it to the "cloud" with your Designer account and sync/load your model into the controller.
Your iPhone App will communicate with the controller. The iPhone is your visualisation application, but this is the controller that sends command to your installation.

Hope this can help

Yann
