How implemented is Zwave? Do some, most or all products work?
Posted by williammanda at Nov 25, 2011 16:23

The first iteration works with HomeScenario IP-to-ZWave gateway. They have a list of Z-wave products they sell with their gateway (few sensor types, switches, Z-Wave to Infrared, etc.)

We are at the moment working on support for Z-Stick from Aeon Labs: http://www.aeon-labs.com/site/products/view/2/

The list of Z-wave products that are known to work at the moment are those supported by HomeScenario. We'll be looking at expanding the list of verified products once additional Z-wave controller integration is finished. In theory, Z-Wave products interoperate but we don't know yet how well that holds true in reality.
