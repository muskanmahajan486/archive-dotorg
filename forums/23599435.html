Comments:

i have a cron job to remove logs and restart every night. This is probably overkill, but i used to suffer from it locking up a lot, and decided i would like it to restart at midnight fresh with no log build up.

The Cron looks like this
15 0 * * * /opt/openremote/scripts/dr

and the script is as follows (remember to chmod +x to set it as executable).

rm /opt/openremote/logs/boot.log

rm /opt/openremote/logs/dev/*

/sbin/shutdown -r now

HTH Nick
