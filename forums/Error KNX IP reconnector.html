All,

I could fix the problem. After having seen, that the same error occurs also when using my iMac as OR host, I focussed again on hardware around the IP-interface. I renewed the bus cabling to the IP-interface's KNX connector, et voil√†: it worked again.

The tricky thing was, that the KNX status LED on the interface indicated that bus connection was fine. There must have been a contact resistance somewhere in the cabling that caused the problem.
