Hi Michal,

I think a starter kit for testing EnOcean technology should include the following components:

  • USB interface
  • Switch
  • Sensor (temperature/humidity, window contact...)
  • Actuator

The cheapest USB interface is the 'BSC EnOcean Smart Connect USB' stick.

My favorite sensors are from TELEFUNKEN (Temperature/Humidity). The TELEFUNKEN devices are in conformance with the EnOcean specification, are compact in size and have a reasonable price. Unfortunately they are only available in germany but I have had good experiences with manketech when ordering.

Last week I've seen that BERKER has some brand new EnOcean switches. I haven't seen them in real life but I'm pretty much sure that they are by far the best EnOcean switches to date. The base module (2411 10 09 or 2412 10 09) can be combined with frames from the S.1/B.3/B.7 series!!

For EnOcean actuators see the following thread: http://www.openremote.org/display/forums/Cannot+find+EnOcean-Controller. The RCM250 is a very compact device but it it's not a dimmer - just a ON/OFF relay. All other actuators which support the F6-02-01 EEP profile should be OK.
