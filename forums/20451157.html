Posted by pz1 at Sep 30, 2012 16:49

Hi,

the firmware was not changes. the delay may have two reasons. (1) The new setpoint will be transferred only when the devices wakes up and this depends on the wakeup interval the user/the software has defined.
(2) the radiator may start moving the valve but it can not be heard. there is a quite complex algorithm in the background that is sometimes moving big parts (noise) or sometimes moving 100 times ad small part ( no noise). This may confuse the user.

Regards

Christian
