Posted by juha at Nov 30, 2010 05:48

Ok, so your toolbar images work fine on Android but not on Apple.

It turns out Apple puts all kinds of rules on what kind of images can be used in the toolbar. And unless you follow these, all you get is empty (nevermind that you're creating a custom user interface for yourself and you couldn't care less about Apple's UI guidelines).

So apparently the only images I am able to get to work with Apple are the ones here: http://glyphish.com/

Apparently they're in Apple approved style (which seems to mean only use white color and alpha in images, anything else is and you get no image at all).

All of this was undocumented on our side.

Sorry for your frustration but thanks for digging up these issues.
Posted by juha at Nov 30, 2010 08:49

Or in other words, Apple wants you to create an image mask instead of the actual image to use in the toolbar, which they then modify to whatever color scheme they want in the toolbar.
