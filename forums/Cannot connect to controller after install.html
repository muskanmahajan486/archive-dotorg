The controller page should show your controller-id (mac address).
Use that and add this in the pro designer controller management window.
After a minute maximum you can update the controller page and it should show that the controller is linked.
You have to restart the controller after the link since the controller only z-wave devices to the designer upon startup.

If you look at the command line output when you start the controller it should say something about your zwave devices see here: http://www.openremote.org/display/docs/Discover+Controller+Z-Wave+Devices

If the link works you should login into your pro account after 1 minute and a popup shows your found zwave devices.
