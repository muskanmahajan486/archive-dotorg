Hi Sarang,

These are the steps you need to follow to enter codes:
You must know the format of your IR codes.
a) GlobalCache format: "sendir, 1:1,1,38109,1,1,342,170,22,21"
b) HEX format: "0000 006C 0000 0022 0156"
c) GC compressed format: "sendir, 1:1,1,38109,1,1,342,170,22,21,22,63 CCCCC"

Use of iLearn from GloblaCache to convert between the formats.

There is some consolidation work required on the IR front.

As it stands now, the Beehive IR database is used when using LIRCD as the sending mechanism.
And the 'import IR files' mechanism from the Pro version is used to ease the creation of IR codes for use with GC/IRTrans senders, importing them from Pronto XCF files.

Easiest solution right now might be if you can find an XCF configuration file (e.g. on RemoteCentral) for the equipment you want to control.
