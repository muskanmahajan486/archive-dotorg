If you want to create your own blog entries to OpenRemote Community blogs page, you can now do so.

First you need to create your own personal space. You can do this from your profile (go to Home -> Profile menu). In your profile page there's a link to create a personal space.

Once you've created a personal space, you should see a link appearing which allows you to create a new blog post.

You can also create and share other pages within your personal space. It's your "wiki within wiki" in a way.

Consider this still a beta feature. If something's not working, appears broken, or feature seems missing, just let us know on this forum or comments.
