I guess you're looking for remote access WITHOUT VPN given your description, because if you establish a VPN connection to your LAN, then no port forwarding or dyndns is required, just access your controller as you would locally over WiFi. That's the goal of a VPN tunnel, being connected as if locally present.

Without VPN, if you do forward port 8080 external to 8080 on 192.168.0.101, then the URL to use for the controller is http://nnnn.dynsdns.org:8080/controller, you still need to indicate the valid port in the controller URL.

If you do try to forward port 80 external to 8080 internal, then the URL you posted above is correct but if at all possible, I would avoid this setup, might lead to issue, would have to investigate.
