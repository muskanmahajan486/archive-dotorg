Everything I have running is on the same LAN and I have one port open on my router to the openremote controller. I could use a VPN but I'm just lazy like that. Routing some protocols over a WAN connection can get tricky.

If I wanted to have the controller talk to a device that was offsite, I would setup a vpn between the two sites.
