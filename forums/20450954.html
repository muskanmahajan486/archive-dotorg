To prevent you are mixing up things, I wouldn't use macro's which bring you to a specific UI for a category. So if you watch TV you create a button in the menu which brings you to a category and corresponding UI: TV or Entertainment. Within the UI of the TV, you can add scenes (macro's/rules). So in case of TV UI, you can add a scene "Movie" (eg. a macro which dimms the lighting to 10% and set's the audio to surround sound, switches off the doorbell). If you would navigate back to the category Lighting, you could switch the light of and safely go back to the category TV, without the Lighting changing...

This would also prevent your iPhone mixing up the choice you made on your iPad.
