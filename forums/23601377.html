You will need to use rules for this. For example (not tested):

rule "count devices on"
when
  Number(n:intValue) from accumulate($x: Event(source matches "Your light sensor name.*", value=="ON"), count($x))
then
  execute.command("Label with text", n+"/10 lights is ON now");
end
