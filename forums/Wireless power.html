Last night I saw a quick commercial on something called the power pad . It's 'wireless power' (god I hate buzzwords). It's a pad that plugs into the wall and you attach a thin device to the back of your phone, ipod, gameboy, whatever and it recharges the unit when you place the device on the pad. Really not magic or anything new if you understand transformers and/or RFID. Just thought it might be of interest to those of us with too many gadgets and not enough outlets. And yes I realize that it shifts the problem from the wall wart to having the correct device on the back of my phone. The closest link I could find was this:
