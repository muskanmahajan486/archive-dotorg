Contributing your Knowledge

Not all contributions take the form of code. Not everyone on this list is a software programmer. A lot of you are installers that want to share their views. OR is such a community. If you are in a position to share knowledge, please take the time to do so. The knowledge base is something we use internally to document findings and help newcomers educate themselves quickly in the field.
