2009-05-01 10:10:58 AM *juha*   1) we are building openremote as an open community effort in contrast to the current industry trend which tends to be proprietary or pay-to-play in terms of specifications and interoperability.
2009-05-01 10:11:21 AM *juha*   2) we believe the current industry state increases hardware costs (a lot of the service is subsidized by higher hardware margin which also means lack of interest in interoperability standards in order to protect that margin). We target off-the-shelf hardware and aim to increase consumer choice by building interoperability and therefore reduce hardware cost
2009-05-01 10:11:49 AM *juha*   3) we believe *high-quality* smart homes should not be exclusive to million-dollar (or pound) homes but available to a larger audience of home-owners. We enable do-it-yourself users with hardware build instructions and open source software. We provide pre-built and pre-installed hardware and software to professional installers and home-owners who look for a ready-to-use product.
2009-05-01 10:12:07 AM *juha*   4) by using open source we remove barriers to user innovation -- do-it-yourself community is free to innovate, prototype and build features into the software. We get superior QA to integrate various HA systems into OpenRemote which furthers our goals of #2 and #3.
